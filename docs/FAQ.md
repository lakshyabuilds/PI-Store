# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## For the Platform Owner (You)

**Q: I have 0 connections and 0 budget. How do I get my first 10 customers?**
A: Because you are selling a "Business in a Box" for just ₹99/mo, you don't need a huge budget. Go to Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. Search for #facelessmarketing, #digitalproducts, #sidehustle. Send Direct Messages (DMs) to small accounts (500 - 5000 followers) who are trying to sell but have bad link-in-bios. 
*Pitch:* "Hey, love your page. I built a tool that gives you a 1-click storefront and DFY products to sell so you don't have to build anything. Want to try it for a month? It's the cost of a coffee."

**Q: Are the PLR products legally safe to distribute?**
A: PLR (Private Label Rights) products are specifically designed to be rebranded and resold. You must ensure you source your base vault items from legitimate PLR databases and respect their licensing terms. 

**Q: What happens if a creator cancels their ₹99/mo subscription?**
A: Their storefront (`/s/their-slug`) will be deactivated, and they will lose access to the catalog. Data is retained so they can reactivate later.

## For the Creators (Your Customers)

**Q: What exactly am I getting for ₹99/month?**
A: You receive instant access to a vault of premium digital products that you can legally call your own, plus a high-converting storefront to sell them on. You don't need to write code, design websites, or film courses.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Currently, we are simulating the checkout. Once Stripe is fully integrated, sales will be processed securely, and funds will be routed directly to your connected bank account (minus standard credit card processing fees).

**Q: Do I have to show my face to sell these?**
A: Absolutely not. Our platform is built specifically for the "Faceless" creator economy. You can run theme pages on social media and link to your store perfectly anonymously.

**Q: Can I use my own domain name?**
A: Currently, all stores are hosted under our fast, secure subdomain infrastructure to ensure zero setup friction. Custom domain support is on our product roadmap.
